HALEY'S GUIDE TO NYC
INTRODUCTION
Welcome to Haley’s guide to New York! My name is Haley, and I am an NYU student living in New York since August of 2022. As a college student living far away from home, I’m always seeking new creative and cultural experiences to make the best out of my time. At first, I found it difficult to find nice places to go every weekend that aren’t cliché tourist destinations or under 21 friendly. After six months of thorough research and going to these places myself, I decided to compile the perfect guide for people like me based on my experiences. If you’re a student with a limited budget, under 21, and always looking for exciting experiences in NYC, you’ve come to the right place! All images of the restaurants and attractions included in this website are my own and a few from my friends(with two exceptions...), and the descriptions are based on my personal experience. Feel free to reference my recommendations to create your own memories in New York <3
