FREE
DUMBO Photo Spot
• 35 Washington St, Brooklyn, NY 11201 •
Take a picture of the iconic Manhattan bridge view in Down Under the Manhattan Bridge Overpass!
Brooklyn Bridge
• New York, NY 10038 •
Walk across the Brooklyn Bridge for amazing views of Manhattan and fresh air. Wear comfy shoes!
78th Precinct
• 65 6th Ave, Brooklyn, NY 11217 •
Calling all Brooklyn99 fans! The 78th precinct is where the exterior of the 99th precinct was filmed.
Brooklyn Heights Promenade
• Montague St &, Pierrepont Pl, Brooklyn, NY 11201 •
Probably my favorite spot in New York. The Brooklyn Heights Promenade is a long platform and pedestrian walkway in Brooklyn Heights. Amazing views, especially at night!
Staten Island Ferry
• New York, NY 10004 •
The Staten Island Ferry is a passenger ferry route operated by the New York City Department of Transportation. Completely free of charge with an amazing view of the Statue of Liberty!
Oculus Center
• 185 Greenwich St LL3110, New York, NY 10006 •
A shopping center by the One World Trade Center. The interior of the Oculus is absolutely stunning.
9/11 Memorial
• 180 Greenwich St, New York, NY 10007 •
The National September 11 Memorial & Museum is a memorial and museum in New York City commemorating the September 11 attacks of 2001.
The Vessel
• 20 Hudson Yards, New York, NY 10001 •
Vessel is a structure and visitor attraction built as part of the Hudson Yards Redevelopment Project in Manhattan.
Grand Central Terminal
• 89 E 42nd St, New York, NY 10017 •
Grand Central Terminal is a commuter rail terminal located in Midtown. Spotted at Grand Central, Serena Van der Woodsen…!
NYPL
• 476 5th Ave, New York, NY 10018 •
The New York Public Library is a public library system in New York City. With nearly 53 million items and 92 locations, the New York Public Library is the second-largest public library in the United States and the fourth-largest in the world.
The High Line
• New York, NY 10011 •
A 1.45-mile-long elevated linear park, greenway, and rail trail created on a former New York Central Railroad spur on the west side of Manhattan.
